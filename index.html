What is GLAS?

GLAS is a non-profit organization aiming to equip young women with a life-skills toolbox to help them succeed and break the glass ceiling.

We host talks and seminars focused around life and professional skills. Our workshops led by professionals tackle subjects such as Cover Letter & CV-writing, mastering interview skills, improving network & presentation skill, and more.

Through our student-run chapters, we empower CEGEP and university students to become leaders within their community.

We also have a newsletter where we provide exclusive access to our workshops, speaker series and more.

Is GLAS for me?

Are you a student looking for advice on career choices? Or maybe someone looking to perfect their interview and CV-writing skills? If yes, then this is place for you!

GLAS welcomes all young women looking to advance their career by developping useful skills, networking with like-minded individuals and gaining the confidence they need.

We strive to offer a supportive and resourceful community where you can get out of your comfort zone and acheive your goals.

Get Involved

As a Chapter Member

For students in high school, CEGEP and university

As a chapter member, you will have the chance to participate in workshops, seminars and bonding events organized by your local peer leaders.

Joining a GLAS chapter allows you to connect and network with the members of your community and learn alongside your peers with no significant committment.

As a Chapter Lead

For students in high school, CEGEP and university

As a chapter lead, you will have the chance to organize workshops, seminars and bonding events organized for your local peers with the assistance of our Program Coordinator.

Leading a GLAS chapter allows you to connect and network with the members of your community, develop your leadership skills in a more involved role.

As a Professional

For experts in any field.

GLAS is always on the lookout for guest speakers and workshop facilitators.

Being a speaker allows you to share your experience and skills to eager students looking to learn.
